Materialism is a form of philosophy that sees matter as crucial part of nature and everythings else in life. The society should not be criticized for being materialistic. It is not good to be the judge of something oneself might be a victim of. 
Criticizing people for being materialistic is not good, it will not encourage people to spend more. When people spend less than they usually do to acqure property, it affects the economy is a negative way. Businesses will not have the chance to grow and so will the economy, many jobs might also be lost. 
Materialism brings about joy. There is joy that one gets from owning property and other expensive things. Criticizing materialism might cause depression and self doubt, this is not good for the mental health of people. 
There are some cases though that criticizing materialism can be good like increase in equality, if people own the same or less material objects or put less emphasis on it they are more likely to treat each other the same. Equality promotes unity in the society. 
Above all criticizing materialism should be avoided since the advantages outweigh the disadvantages. Materialism improves the well being of people and those around them and it makes life much easier and more enjoyable.
