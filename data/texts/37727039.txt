Images and impressions have an effect on people to a greater extend. Most of the people select the product they need only based on the image and impression they see on the advertisements either on the store or online.Not everyone who buy a product or service , knows much about their requirement and the selection they make. They just buy the product only based on the advertisement and the product presentation.
Now a days advertisement plays a major role in Marketing. It influencs people to think that the product they see in the advertisement is worth buying and has a trustworthy quality. Technology development has a great impact on all fields. Thus the Manufacturers handle different techniques to make customers buy their products or services. Colorful packages, free gifts and offers makes consumers to change their choice of product. 
Appearance of the product is equally important to the quality of the product. A better package , a better design or even a better color choice would change the consumers preference to a graeter extend. The size, color, quality , easy accessability, attractivity of the design, brand name and quantity are the key features explained in the advertisements. But not all the products are good enough to satisfy the consumer. Enough research is required to select a product with good quality at an affordable price.
