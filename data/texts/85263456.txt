
Human beings are extremely visual and social beings. It is only natural for us to want to fit in among those who surround us in our daily lives. Our opinions and worldviews always come from something else, whether it be culture, family, friends, entertainment, or even the institutions we attend. We are what we surround ourselves with, and who or what we choose to be around will ultimately shape us.
 
This fact has a major influence on the products that people buy. Even if a product is all the rage because of a new trend, but its contents are questionable, people will buy it to be part of that trend. The advertisements and promotions of products by celebrities also have a major impact on the market of consumption. Human beings can definately be related to herd animals. When one member of the group changes direction, the others around it will follow. Many individuals adopt this behavior without even thinking about where the situation will eventually lead them. Politicians, if they are successful, have an incredible power to persuade populations to vote for them. A person's country, region, state, city of origin, etc., will greatly shape the types of people they vote into office. 

It is very important for thinking beings, such as humans, to think for ourselves and make our own decisions. Throughout our lives we are encouraged to become our own, unique person. This should be a persepective everyone should be intentional about, and we should encourage one another to be the best person we can be.
