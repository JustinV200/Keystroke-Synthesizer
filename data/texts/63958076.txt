In every task of our life , we will be come across two of the important factors  which are  Competetion and cooperation.  Be it during our child hood,  teenage or adulthood - we always have to go through the competetion and the cooperation at all the stages of our life.

Every individual have their own opinion about which one is more important to suceed in life.  When observed,  for example - at business , when we are starting a business , at the foremost we would initially need the cooperation to move forward and establish the business  at a level. Once the business is established, then we need to look for the competetion to be stable in the market at business.

Competetion keeps the business running and be better. Competetion makes us to be ahead of others and be updated with the market trends which will lead to the road of success.
With the right competetor in the business , we will be making no mistakes and be moving in the right direction towards our goal as indirectly, our competetor will be pushing us to not stay in the same place because updates in the business becomes unavoidable. Certainly, cooperation with the right people inside the company or business will be an added advantage for the success of the business.  Competetion and cooperation go hand in hand. 
Creativity , inspiration and challenges are the outcome of the competetion.  Better understanding, patience and effective solutions are derived from the cooperation. Success is definitely based on both the factors. They are interdependent. Both factors drive people towards their ideas , skills or performances  and make them become the best possible person.
