Happiness is everywhere. You don't need to go and find. All you need is to take a closer look. Happiness comes from within. It doesn't depend on anything but you. To be happy, first we should live our life the way we wanted it to be. Happiness is a feeling that to be feeled by us. 

Some people more likely to be happy when they focus on their dreams. They intended to do all the works to achieve their dreams. They will work hard with much enthusiasm. They wont get tired of what they are doing. They feel happy doing everything to achieve their dream. They feel like they are happy. But they don't even realize that after attaining whatever they are dreaming of, the happiness wont stay long as the same at the beginning. This is because, they simply running for their dreams while failed to enjoy all the moments.

Some people stays happy almost all the way they are travelling. They will enjoy each and everything on their way. they accept all their success and failures as same. They knew that nothing cost much then being sad when we can be happy. They love being so kind to people.

Thinking about others happiness leads a beautiful life. It'll bring beauty to one's life. Making someone happy isn't as easy as we think. It need patience, abundant love and a lot of self confidence. One can be happy, by making others happy. And this happiness stays forever.
