What is happiness? Whether it is inside us or it comes from outside? Everyone will have their own answer. It depends upon every indivudal. Now let us discuss whether happiness will come by pursuing our personal goal or making others happy?
We all will have one or more personal goals to achieve in our life time .It may be big or small. But we strive hard to achieve these goals in our life time. It may be in our exams or to become rich or to travel to our favorite destination. whatever it may be when we achieve our goal it gives us immense satisfaction  fulfillment and self-esteem.
Next let us discuss about making others happy. Not only by giving money or things we can make others happy. If someone is in trouble if we stand by their side it makes them happy. A small help will mean a lot to a needy person.  By helping others we can reduce our stress. If we spend our time with a lonely person, it  will make them very happy. In the smile of a needy person we can see the God.  Help may be big or small it depends upon the needy person. 
Although it gives immense pleasure and fulfillment while achieving our personal goals, the real happiness is  making others  happy. So try to make others happy.
