Materialism cannot be seen as a bad thing in a total way. Being too materialistics can be argued to promote selfishness but it can also be argued to promote working hard. To achieve wanted material possessions, individual has to be able to afford them, thereby-working hard. 
Selfish behaviours can also arise from being too materialistic, only caring about oneself. That could lead to achieving goals through immoral or illegal means. 
Materialism can be argued to be the reason for an increase in suicide rates across the world. When people feel they should be able to own fashionable cars, spend lavishly, and own big houses- inability to achieve those material things can easily propend an individual to start contemplating suicide. Reasons arising from seeing their peers possessing the material things and they could not. 
Materialism in modern days has significantly increased because of the internet. Internet has made it very easy to have access to different informations at any given time by just googling. Although, modern society should definitely be criticised for being too materialistic as it enforces nagative behaviors among individuals. It cannot totally be termed a bad thing also as it improves individuals commitment to working harder, to make it in life in order to be able to afford the material possessions.
