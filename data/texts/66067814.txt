What is happiness? Happiness can mean a variety of things to different people. Happiness can mean having a lot of money or being with people you love. 

There are many ways to experience happiness. Sometimes we can create our happiness and sometimes others can create it for us. One of the best ways to for people to be happy is to focus the happiness of others.

Focusing on the happiness of others will bring joy because people always need help and it is rewarding to be in a position to help others. When you focus on personal goals to achieve happiness, you may become disapponted if you do not reach those goals. You will never be disappointed because you helped another person. 

You may have a goal to save a thousand dollars by the end of the month and not obtain it. It may cause you to feel depressed and doubt yourself in the future. If your goal is to help someone have a better day it will always be acheivable and you will feel good about yourself everytime. 

Happiness is something people experience everyday. Some people may feel happiness just from waking up everyday, while for others there happiness comes from what they have acheived that day. Doing something to bring happiness to others will always cause you to be happy as well.
