Yes, images and appearances have too much of an effect on people. We see several new brands that are being promoted in social media platforms. As a user sees those advertisements repeatedly the brand name is registered in the mind of the user. This creates an illusion that it is a well established brand and people do not hesitate to buy the products of that brand. These advertisements continuously create trust among lots and lots of people and grow their business.
Several tactics such as free samples, share this advertisement with your friends and earn money kind of tactics attract crowd and their brand name reaches more and more people.
In departmental stores, attractive products are arranged near the entrance and near the billing counter so that the crowd is interested in purchasing those products. And they do succeed in selling more of those products.
Visual images are registered in people's mind easily rather than words or description. That is the reason for the success of today's social media. One needs to present his works and products in a more attractive way. Working hard but not letting the people know what you are doing through social media doesn't bring succcess in today's world. the end.
