Some people say money doesn't bring happiness, or that happiness can only come from within and we should'nt rely on others to make us happy. So if any of that true or all of it? Do we look for happiness in all the wrong places when in fact we have the ability to make ourselves happy. And if so, then when we feel happy to see others happy are we wrong for that? Or is it a problem when instead we put others happiness before our own causing us to be unhappy? 

I believe that happiness is different for all of us. It's not a 1 size fits all, and what may make me happy, might not  do the same for you and vise versa. Thats where balance comes in, if you are so busy putting in more effort to make someone else happy you are more than likely taking away from your own happiness.  With that being said its not always a bad thing to put others before ourselves, but if it becomes a habit and is taking away from your own happiness thats when it becomes an issue.
 
I have learned that doing things for the people I love, makes me happy. Im not putting anyones needs before my own all the time or feel obligated to do so. I do what makes me happy and sometimes that means putting myself first as well. So from my experience Ive learned that I dont need to rely on anyone else to make me happy however I enjoy doing things that make others happy.
