  Success is something that everyone desire in their life. But the key point is what drived you to success. Is it a healthy competition or a undeniable cooperation? People have different views about success. The path or way of proceedings may be different but the goal is the same.
  
  Since we know the reaching point is the same, its time to decide which path is gonna take us there safely and which path is healthy. Some people choose competion as a tool for success where you have the desire and a sparkle in the battle field. It might hinder one's action even more. It act as a catalyst in achieving the goal. The qustion is "is it healthy?". It may let you to reach the success you desire but not in the good shape most of the times. It might hinder the hatred for the competitor along the way.

  One of the best way to achieve success is by cooperation. It may seem like a diificult path in the beggining but once the person choose the path it is a straight road ahead. The way is so pure and healthy, In the way we can encounter some valuable things like friendship, toleration, helping tendency. It would be the best way to travel to achieve success.

   In conclution, it always depends on the person who is going to travel along the way. The best should always be decided by the people who travelled those paths before.
