Everyone on the planet wants to be successful in his/her own way.  In general, people achieve success in a variety of  ways.  Some people are driven by competition while others are driven by cooperation.  Competition  causes rivalry.  Someone will win and someone will lose.  Cooperation allows everyone to win.  Cooperation provides a safe space for all to pitch in and participate.  Competition is just the opposite.  One person feels like the winner and the others feel like losers.  Unfortuantly, most people don't believe in the theory of it's not whether you win or lose it's how you play the game.  


Cooperation is the the best way to achieve long lasting success. Everyone is not competive.  On the other hand, most everyone needs to feel connected to other people.  Connection and cooperation fosters our mental, physical, and emotional well-being.  Competion isolates individuals for his/her success.  Only one person will be the top producer.  Cooperation allows everyone to do his/her best and reap the benefits of a group effort.  Cooperation allows for strenghts and weakness.  Everyone has strenghts and weaknesses.  Cooperation allows for everyone to use his/her strenghts in a given situation.  At the end of the day, cooperation promotes an excepting atmosphere vs a competive atmosphere.
