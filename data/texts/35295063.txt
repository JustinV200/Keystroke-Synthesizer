People find happiness from their day to day life. They don't need to go and search for it. Whatever a person do, they will find happiness in it. Happiness is inevitable. Everything we do with intension, gives happiness. Whether it is good or bad, people will be happy doing that.

For some people, happiness is doing their own job, persuing their dream, getting their dream job, building a home etc., Everything depends on their own need. They will work for their dream and be happy attaining them.

But for some other people, happiness is helping others, making others happy, putting smile on others face when they are at their tough times. Giving what they have to others, gives them much happiness. They will always try to help people irrespective of their needs. They are givers. They give and get happy. 

Both type of people get happiness in their own ways. But the real happiness comes to those who give much love and happiness to others. It's like what we give, we get. When we give happiness, we get double the amount of happiness in return. This is how it calculates.

Happiness can be attained anywhere irrespective of time, place and other factors. All we need is some pure heart to be happy at everytime. Either we do good to ourself or others, doing good things will always bring happiness to one's life.
