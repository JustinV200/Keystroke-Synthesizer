People believe that happiness comes by satisfying their desires such as chasing their dreams, having fun in entertainment etc. But the real happiness lies in satisfying one's inner self. Happiness attained through fun factors are temporary. On the other hand, happiness that satisfies one's inner self gives a inner pleasure which keeps on lasting forever. It creates happiness even when you think of those instances that created inner happiness.

Such inner happiness, comes from making others happy.The Same happiness can be attained by working on something that helps in development and well being of humanity on the larger scale. Happiness achieved through these two reasons would not only give heppiness to one's self, but also to the one's who gained help through such act. Moreover, this sense of happiness even gives a positive attitude towards one's life.

Inner happiness, not only ends in a day or two. It actually expands, on each and every day when you recall those beautiful yet meaningful past. Simply, inner happiness that is obtained through helping others or improving humanity will make the upcoming generations to work and act in the same way as their ancestors, which on the other hand motivates them to do the same. This selfless love will keep on spreading until humanity exists.
