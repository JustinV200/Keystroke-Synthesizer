Happiness comes from many sources in life. These  may include interactions with  family, friends and work associates. However, indivuduals are most happy when they find enjoyment in another's happiness. But how can this high level of happiness be achieved? Let's look at what research has found.  

As humans we want to be happy. We try to find activities that give us enjoyment and happiness. These activities may include family gatherings, parties with friends and work tasks or employment.  Research has found that most happy individuals find satifivastion and enjoyment by focusing finding happiness in other. This may include volunteering in schools, helping a non-profit, or contribuing financially to causes that are most enjoyed by an individual. Time, talents and treasures can be used to achieve a high level of happiness and enjoyment.  

Studies have proven that happy individuals have on the average 3.6 activities that bring them happiness. These actitivies should involve an element of socializing. Examples include playing tennis, church groups, card clubs, gym memberships, and other both physical and social activities. Individuals who engage in these types of activities have proven to have long and happy lives.   

As an individual thinks about their life path, they should include making others happy by using their time, talents and financial treasures. By committing to these principles their level of happiness will expand, goals will be accomplished and their level of success will be achieved.
